Self-Care

“Our bodies are our garden to which our wills are gardeners.” -- William Shakespeare

The Importance of Self-Care

Self-care is taking care of yourself physically, mentally, and emotionally. It is often seen or perceived as being selfish, but in reality it is helping you live in the moment and enjoy life. This is important because in order for you to help others you must first help yourself.

Self-Care Routine

Maintaining a routine can be difficult. Even more when you have lots of responsabilities. So, in order to begin a self-care routine you first need to find out what are those areas where you need most care.

Challenges

One of the hardest things when it comes to self-care is taking the time to actually do it. Just like a garden takes watering, sunlight, and oxygen, so do we. We need a daily input to grow as people, to give our 100%.

Types of Self-care

There are various types of self-care, but the main three are emotion, mental, and physical.
